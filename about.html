Patrick Brock

     Hi, I'm Patrick, I try to convey the aspects of life that I enjoy through a number of mediums. I'm fascinated with the kind of raw nebulous emotion that we feel in our dreams. Quiet moments where through some vaguely familiar sensation you find a nearly forgotten memory. Most of my work is just an attempt in some form to encapsulate and reproduce those feelings.
